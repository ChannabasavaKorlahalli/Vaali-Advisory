About the firm

Traditional rigor, rebuilt for crypto finance.

Vaali Advisory was created for operators who need a finance partner that understands token mechanics, treasury complexity, governance models, and the realities of closing the books in a fast-moving blockchain environment.

What we believe

Clear accounting is a growth function. The right reporting, controls, and documentation unlock trust with investors, regulators, banking partners, auditors, and boards.

Finance-first execution

We build around close discipline, reporting quality, and evidence readiness rather than only post-hoc cleanup.

Crypto-native literacy

Wallet flows, bridges, rollups, staking, custody, and protocol governance are part of the operating conversation from the start.

Practical partnership

Our work is designed to reduce drag on your internal team and create usable systems, not just presentations.

How we work

A model built for leaders who need speed and control.

  • Initial discovery focused on transaction flows, systems, ownership, and reporting obligations.
  • Rapid issue triage to identify policy decisions, reconciliation gaps, and control blind spots.
  • Execution sprints aligned to close cycles, audit milestones, and board or investor reporting windows.
Operating principle
We want the CFO, controller, auditor, and operating team to be able to point to the same numbers, same evidence, and same process map without friction.
